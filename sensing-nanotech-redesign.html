Research & Impact

Satish Nagarajaiah and his collaborators are among the earliest to invent and develop strain sensing using nanomaterials — both contact and noncontact laser-based — through an institute funded by NASA (Texas Institute of Intelligent Bio-Nano Materials and Structures for Aerospace Vehicles, TiiMS; co-principal investigator).

Nagarajaiah and collaborators conducted widely cited research on nanotube-based strain sensing in an AFOSR-funded study. In close collaboration with Bruce Weisman (Chemistry, Rice University), the team developed and demonstrated laser-based non-contact strain sensing with funding from the Office of Naval Research.

Commercialization · 2024
